Research

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Earth Sciences Department at the University of Southern California and a Graduate Student Intern at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory. My work utilizes airborne platforms, the international space station (ISS), and satellite platforms accompanied by extensive field campaigns to link surface mineral spectroscopy and remote detection of heavy metal to subsurface processes.
